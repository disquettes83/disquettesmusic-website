disquette•s

An Italian Chillwave/Retrowave solo act. A passionate digger of more or less obscure records from the past, ranging from punk, post-punk, new wave, synth pop, alternative rock, and videogame music to the cheesiest italo disco and eurodance. All of it, in one way or another, influences his own productions.

Started messing around with computer music as a kid on a glorious Commodore Amiga 600 in the mid-'90s. Got more serious with trackers, then moved on to Fruity Loops in the early 2000s. Has been producing professionally for over 15 years.
