about disquette•s

Italian producer crafting electronic music since 2005. With a unique blend of Chillwave, New Wave, Synth-pop, and Italo-disco influences, disquette•s creates immersive sonic landscapes that transport listeners to neon-lit streets and retro-futuristic worlds.

Each production features meticulously crafted sounds and arrangements that balance nostalgic elements with contemporary production techniques.
