working together

And here we are, faced with a list of serious things.

  • Brief Chat

    We'll start with a quick discussion to understand your project, goals, and creative vision. This helps establish clear expectations and direction from the beginning.

  • Schedule & Scope

    We'll set a timeline that works for both of us and define exactly what you'll receive. This includes deliverables, revisions, and any specific requirements for your project.

  • Production

    During the creation process, I'll send progress updates at key points to ensure we're on the right track. This maintains alignment with your vision throughout development.

  • Feedback & Refinement

    You'll review the work and provide feedback, and I'll make necessary adjustments. This collaborative stage ensures the final result matches your expectations.

  • Delivery

    Once approved, you'll receive the final files in the formats you need for your specific use case, whether it's for streaming, vinyl pressing, or multimedia projects.
